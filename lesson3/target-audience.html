Target Audience

The target audience for this website is residents of the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. It is intended for adults or teens of all ages that need to know their local weather as well as events happening in the town or other local information.

2 Personas:

Jane Doe

Jane is an eighteen year old high school student. She goes to school in Preston and drives herself to school everyday. She enjoys using her phone for social media and other apps such as Apple News, various phone games, texting and Whatsapp. She enjoys hanging out with her friends on the weekends and is always looking for fun new things to do.

  1. Jane would check the site before heading to school in the morning to check what clothing to wear.
  2. Jane would check the site to check for any traffic closures especially in the winter to make sure the roads are safe before driving.
  3. This person would check the site for any local events happening in the town to see what fun things they could go to on the weekends.

John Doe

John is a 40 year old married adult. He lives with his wife and 3 children ages 10, 6, and 3. He has a 20 minute commute to work everyday from his suburban home. He enjoys playing with his kids and creating memories with them. He and his wife take his kids to different fun places in the city. He is an active citizen in his community and participates in local voting and programs at his children's school.

  1. This person would check the site before going to work in the morning and checks the traffic.
  2. This person would check the site to be aware of any local events happening that they might want to take their children to. This could include fairs, food trucks, Easter egg hunts, etc.
  3. This person would check the site to be aware of any community issues such as information about local school levies, recycling programs, school closures due to weather, etc.
