Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to show the local weather in the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. In addition to the weather, it will also act as a community page where events in the community are displayed. It will have pages for the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.
