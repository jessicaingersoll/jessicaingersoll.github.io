Soda Springs Blood Drive

Soda Springs will be holding their monthly blood drive on August 1st in the local highschool gymnasium. Please consider donating to help all those in our community who need this life saving service. We will be providing food and water for all those who donate. The community is trying to put on blood drives every month to support the local hospitals. All phlebotomists will follow safety protocols including wearing medical gloves and changing them after each donation, disinfecting the equipment and areas touched, and using sterilized collection sets. Temperature checks will also be administered before the donor will be allowed to donate.
Do you know these fun facts from the American Red Cross?

  • Every 2 seconds someone in the US needs blood and or platelets.
  • Approximately 36,000 units of red blood cells are needed every day in the US.
  • Nearly 7,000 units of platelet and 10,000 units of plasma are needed daily in the US.
  • Less than 38% of the population is eligible to give blood or platelets.
  • A single car accident victim can require as many as 100 units of blood.
  • One donation can potentially save up to three lives.
  • Blood and platelets cannot be manufactured; they can only come from volunteer donors
Source: Red Cross Blood Facts
