Our Chamber

Our Chamber is an organization that fosters positive relationships in the Miami Beach business community. The Chamber has regular social events where members can meet and build networks. We also hold business development seminars to further the education of our members in relevant areas such as modern social media marketing, tax tips, and more. Miami Beach is a special city that has unique family businesses. We want to help foster your small business and help it be the best it can be for you and our community.
